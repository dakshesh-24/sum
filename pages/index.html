Introduction

Welcome to our Text Summarizer Application! This tool helps you quickly summarize long pieces of text into concise, readable summaries. Whether you're looking to shorten articles, reports, or any other text, our tool is designed to make the process easy and efficient.

How It Works

  1. Go to the Summarize Text page.
  2. Paste or type the text you want to summarize.

  4. Receive your summarized text instantly.
